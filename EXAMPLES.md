## How These Reports Are Used

1. **Submission to Authorities**
   - Print and submit in person
   - Email to concerned department
   - Upload to government portal
   - Reference for follow-up

2. **Tracking**
   - Keep copy for personal records
   - Reference when following up
   - Evidence if issue recurs

3. **Data Analysis**
   - Authorities track common issues
   - Plan maintenance schedules
   - Allocate resources efficiently

4. **Community Building**
   - Shows citizen engagement
   - Builds accountability
   - Encourages same from others

## Tips for Effective Reports

✓ **Location**: Be as specific as possible - include landmarks, pole numbers, etc.
✓ **Description**: Describe what you see, not opinions. Actual measurements help.
✓ **Severity**: Be honest - don't exaggerate, but highlight safety concerns.
✓ **Evidence**: Mention available photos/videos
✓ **Contact**: Providing contact allows follow-up (optional but recommended)

## Department Response Timeline

- **Within 24 hours**: Acknowledgment
- **Within 3-7 days**: Initial assessment
- **Within 2-4 weeks**: Repair/action

If no response, follow up with a copy of your report!
